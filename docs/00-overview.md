# 1HP — Project Overview

## What is 1HP?

**1HP** stands for **One Human Powered**.

1HP is a platform for human-powered movement. It serves people who run, walk, cycle, swim, and participate in triathlons — treating the human body as one continuous system rather than separate sport silos.

**Domain**: 1HP.in

---

## The Three Pillars

| Pillar | Route | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Participate** | `/participate` | Find human-powered events |
| **Equip** | `/equip` | Gear that lasts |
| **Understand** | `/runstate` | Know where you are |

Each pillar stands alone. Together, they form a system.

---

## What 1HP Is

- A home for human-powered events
- A place to equip yourself thoughtfully
- A system to understand your body
- A long-term companion to movement

## What 1HP Is Not

- A fitness social network
- A performance marketplace
- A coaching or motivation platform
- A leaderboard of bodies
- A place to prove anything

---

## Core Beliefs

1HP believes:

- Movement is not a competition by default
- The body is not a machine to be exploited
- Consistency matters more than extremes
- Humans move for many reasons, not just speed
- Walking counts
- Rest counts
- Aging counts
- Returning counts

1HP rejects:

- "No pain, no gain"
- Hustle culture applied to the body
- Ranking humans by output
- Reducing movement to numbers alone
- Turning participation into pressure

---

## Runstate

Runstate is the intelligence layer within the Understand pillar.

It exists because:
- Activity alone is not understanding
- Data alone is not meaning
- The body must be treated as one system

Runstate:
- Explains, never pushes
- Interprets, never judges
- Observes, never ranks

Runstate tells you **where you are**, not what to do.
